## Overview

Communities face various challenges throughout the year, from seasonal needs during Ramadan to emergency situations requiring immediate support. Our Emergency & Seasonal Aid program provides timely assistance to families in crisis and during significant periods.

## What We Do

*   **Seasonal Food Packages**: Comprehensive food baskets during Ramadan and other critical times, containing rice, lentils, oil, sugar, dates, and other essentials.
*   **Emergency Relief**: Rapid response support for families facing unexpected crises or disasters.
*   **Zakat Management**: We collect and distribute Zakat funds strictly according to Islamic principles to eligible recipients.

## Impact Goals

Our emergency and seasonal aid program aims to:

*   Provide food packages to families during Ramadan and times of need
*   Respond to emergency situations with timely assistance
*   Manage Zakat distribution according to Islamic principles

Impact metrics will be documented and published as the program progresses.
