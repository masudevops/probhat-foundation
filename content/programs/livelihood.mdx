## Overview

Probhat Foundation is committed to fostering economic independence within the community. Our livelihood programs are designed to transition vulnerable families away from dependency by providing them with the tools, skills, and resources necessary to build sustainable, self-reliant futures.

## Key Initiatives

*   **Income-Generating Asset Support**: Provision of physical assets such as rickshaws, small business stalls, or livestock to help families establish immediate sources of income.
*   **Women’s Skills Training**: Structured vocational programs focusing on tailoring, handicrafts, and other viable skills to empower women with independent earning potential.
*   **Micro-Enterprise Assistance**: Small-scale assistance and guidance for community members to start or improve micro-enterprises.
*   **Sustainable Income Initiatives**: Long-term support programs focused on financial literacy and gradual economic stabilization for families in transition.

## Economic Impact

By focusing on empowerment rather than just relief, we aim to create a cycle of self-sufficiency that benefits not only individual families but the community as a whole.
