## Overview

Probhat Foundation believes that education is the most effective tool for lasting community development. Our approach provides a balanced focus on both formal academic excellence and the preservation of essential community values.

## Academic Initiatives

*   **School Supplies Distribution**: Providing essential learning materials including notebooks, supplies, and uniforms to students in need.
*   **Tuition Assistance Programs**: Structured financial support for families to ensure children remain enrolled in formal education.
*   **Youth Scholarship Fund**: Merit-based financial aid for high-achieving students pursuing higher education goals.
*   **Orphan Education Support**: Dedicated support packages ensuring continuous educational access for orphaned children.

## Values-Based Learning

In addition to formal academic support, the foundation facilitates access to faith-based educational resources and structured learning programs for children and adults.

*   **Faith-Based Resources**: Distribution of the Qur’an and essential educational books to students and community members.
*   **Learning Programs**: Facilitating access to structured Qur’an teaching programs and basic Islamic education, designed to foster ethical development and community cohesion.

## Our Philosophy

We aim to produce well-rounded individuals who are equipped with both the knowledge needed for professional success and the values necessary for responsible citizenship.
