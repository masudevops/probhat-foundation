## Overview

Probhat Foundation provides structured assistance to vulnerable families during seasonal periods of need and in response to unforeseen emergencies. Our relief programs are designed to maintain the dignity of every recipient while ensuring efficient and transparent distribution.

## Key Initiatives

*   **Ramadan & Eid Support**: Distribution of essential food packages and Eid gifts to ensure families can observe these periods with security and joy.
*   **Qurbani Meat Distribution**: Organized meat distribution to underserved communities during Eid-ul-Adha.
*   **Winter Assistance**: Structured distribution of high-quality blankets and warm clothing to protect families during the cold season.
*   **Emergency Relief**: Rapid-response assistance for families facing immediate crises or natural disasters in local communities.

## Program Implementation

All relief initiatives follow a structured process of beneficiary identification, internal documentation, and community-verified distribution to ensure that support reaches those who need it most.
