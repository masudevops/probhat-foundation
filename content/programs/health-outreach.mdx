## Overview

Probhat Foundation facilitates access to essential healthcare in underserved areas through organized medical outreach. Our health programs focus on providing basic clinical services and fostering health awareness within the community.

## Key Initiatives

*   **Quarterly Medical Clinics**: Regularly scheduled free clinics where volunteer medical professionals provide basic health assessments and consultations.
*   **Medication Support**: Provision of essential prescriptions and basic medications to patients who lack the means to procure them.
*   **Health Awareness Sessions**: Structured workshops focusing on hygiene, nutrition, disease prevention, and maternal health to improve long-term community wellness.

## Our Approach

We coordinate with local healthcare providers and volunteers to ensure that our outreach is medically sound, ethically managed, and targeted toward those with the greatest barriers to health access.
