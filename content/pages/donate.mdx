# Make a Difference Today

Every donation, no matter the size, helps us bring meaningful support to communities in need.

## Choose a Fund

Select the type of donation you want to support:

- **General Fund** — Education, healthcare, and community development  
- **Zakat Fund** — Distributed to eligible beneficiaries according to Islamic charitable principles  

## How Your Support Helps

Your contribution helps us plan and execute initiatives with clarity, documentation, and accountability. As we grow, we will publish structured impact updates and reports.

## Payment Methods

Use one of the methods below to contribute. If you need the latest details for Zelle or Bangladesh local methods, please reach out through the Contact page.

## Transparency

We take accountability seriously. Funds are tracked internally by category (General vs Zakat), and documentation is maintained for program execution and reporting.
